Our Story

Shawnee's Premiere
Rustic Wedding Barn

Nestled along Clear Pond Road in Bethel Acres, Oklahoma, Little C Ranch is an authentic rustic barn venue — a blank canvas where you can create everything you've envisioned. With décor items available on-site, personal guidance from owner Curtis, and a breathtaking setting beneath ancient oak trees, your day will be as unique as your love story.
